About
“I like to travel the world to find inspiration for my music and give people a sense of peace and joyfulness through the sounds I’m making every week.”

Sevenkey is a Colombian DJ and producer, named “Best Breakthrough DJ Producer” at the 2016 Colombia Dance Awards. Rooted in the captivating melodies of Afro House, House and Indie Dance, he has carried his sound from Ibiza to Lapland, Santiago, Punta Cana and Tanzania.

Released on labels like Sony Music, Loudkult and Soave — and championed by Lost Frequencies, Dash Berlin and The Chainsmokers — his music has reached nearly 8 million listeners worldwide.
